Hamari Kahani

Kismat se likhi ek dastan...

Kabhi kabhi, sabse khoobsurat kahaniyan do parivaron ke milan se shuru hoti hain. Hamari kahani bhi kuch aisi hi hai. Jab hamare parivaron ne hamein milaya, to hum ek anjaan safar par nikle the.

Pehli mulakat ki jhijhak se lekar ek doosre ko samajhne tak ka safar kab pyaar mein badal gaya, pata hi nahi chala. Humne is rishtey mein vishwas, samman aur ek mazboot dosti paayi hai. Ab hum is dosti ko zindagi bhar ke saath mein badalne ja rahe hain aur is khaas mauke par aapka aashirwad chahte hain.
